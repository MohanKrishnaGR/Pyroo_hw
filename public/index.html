Fire Safety SOP & Guidelines

  1. Immediate Actions
    • Stay calm and assess the situation
    • Activate the nearest fire alarm
    • Call emergency services (101)
    • Evacuate the area immediately
  2. During Evacuation
    • Use designated evacuation routes
    • Do not use elevators
    • Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation
    • Assist others if safe to do so
  3. Assembly Point
    • Proceed to designated assembly area
    • Do not re-enter the building
    • Wait for emergency personnel
    • Report any missing persons

Electrical Safety
  • Regularly inspect electrical wiring
  • Avoid overloading power outlets
  • Use surge protectors
  • Unplug unused appliances
Fire Extinguisher Use
  • P.A.S.S. Method:
    • Pull the pin
    • Aim at the base
    • Squeeze the handle
    • Sweep side to side

  • Maintain clear emergency exits
  • Regular fire safety inspections
  • Proper storage of flammable materials
  • Functioning smoke detectors
  • Accessible fire extinguishers
  • Clear evacuation routes

  1. Immediate Reporting
    • Call emergency services (101)
    • Provide exact location
    • Describe the situation
    • Follow operator instructions
  2. Documentation
    • Record incident details
    • Note any injuries
    • Document property damage
    • Submit official report
